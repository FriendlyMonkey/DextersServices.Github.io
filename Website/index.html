About

I’m a low poly 3D modeler and UI designer with a focus on clean, efficient design for games. I use Blender to create stylized environments, props, and assets that match each game’s vibe. For interface design, I work in Figma to build clear and intuitive UIs that enhance the player experience.

Whether it’s modeling or UI work, I aim to deliver high-quality results that fit seamlessly into your project.
